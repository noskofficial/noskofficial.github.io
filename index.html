About The Klub

NOSK - Nepal Open Source Klub is a vibrant community of like-minded individuals dedicated to raising awareness and adoption of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) in Nepal. We believe in the power of open collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Our mission is to educate and train people across different domains of technology by organizing workshops, training sessions, and community events that make FOSS accessible to everyone.

Founded by Nitesh Rijal on February 28th, 2008, NOSK began as the NCIT Open Source Klub and has since grown into Nepal's premier FOSS community, fostering innovation and collaboration.
